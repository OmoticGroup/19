Background Image in Login Page

The Background Image in Login Page module allows users to customize the login screen by setting a background image. Make your login page visually appealing and unique with ease.

Set Background Image in Login Page

To set a Background Image for your Odoo login page, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Users & Companies > Companies.
  2. Select the company for which you want to set the login background.

Set Company Image

To set the Company Image for login background display, follow these steps:

  1. In the General Information tab, locate the Image field.
  2. Upload your desired image in this field.
  3. Click Save to apply the changes.

See Your Login Page Background

After setting the company image, navigate to the Login Page. You will see your chosen image applied as the background, giving a personalized look to your Odoo login.


Key Features

Allows setting a custom background image for the Odoo login page.
Enhances user experience by giving a personalized and professional look to your login screen.
Quick and easy configuration through Company settings without coding.
