Overview

This module enables you to seamlessly embed local video content on your Odoo website through a user-friendly drag-and-drop snippet feature. Ideal for showcasing tutorials, product features, and promotional content using your own video files.

Features

Hassle-Free Setup

Straightforward installation with a step-by-step guide.

Drag-and-Drop Functionality

Effortlessly add video snippets to your pages with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface.

Host Videos Locally

Supports local MP4 files, enabling videos to be stored on your server.
